Apsas

Client : School project/Apsas organisation
Année : 2015
Type de projet : Web design

School project for a real client, the Apsas organisation, which try to raise awareness about animals thought as "problematic" and "dangerous", but are now endangered, such as the fox in France. I had to design a website and its identity, to replace an old website that doesn't inspire people to contact and support Apsas. The website had to be visually enticing, and easily readable, as there is a lot of information on the animals. The contact page is important as it is a way of letting people know that they are active on social medias and call for support for their cause.
