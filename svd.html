SVD Internship

Client : SVD L'agence Digitale
Année : 2015
Type de projet : Motion design & graphic design

During summer 2015 I took an internship of 4 months inside the SVD agency, specialised in Motion Design for in-firm communication and 4x3 giant screens around France, mostly around entry and exit points of cities.
I spent four months doing mostly shorts spots (around 10 seconds) for differents companies, more or less known, mostly through the group Exterion Media. SVD was also the first in France that created a Motion spot for multiple screens at the same time, like the six screens installed on the highway to inside Paris.
The project was comissioned by TomTom for their TomTom GO line of products, and I had the chance to work on it with another employee of SVD. Here is a short presentation of the work I did inside this agency.
