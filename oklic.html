Oklic Internship

Client : Oklic
Année : 2016
Type de projet : Graphic design, newletters, Web devloppement

During my last year at University, I had to take an internship, and I chose to do a six months internship at Oklic, a SEO & Web agency.
Here is part of the work I did for them, mainly the web communication about the new products from their clients, their promotions, and the creation of a new template for the newsletter of PimPomPerles.fr, a fantasy jewelry store.
I also renewded the graphic part of PimPomPerles.fr online communication towards their clients, from their mails to social medias, with new photographs and visuals. Another thing I made were infographics for Oklic's clients after receiving the stats of the customers purchases habits.
