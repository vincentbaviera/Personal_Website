Mont du Forez

Client : School Project / Regional counsel
Année : 2016
Type de projet : Editorial design & Web design

University project where each student was competiting over the first place, to get to finish the selected proposal for the client. Our job was to re-designe and organize a cultural & practical guide for farmers in the Forez, a region in France, about the different kinds of vegetation there is and their properties, to know if they are harmless or benefic for the cattle. I then redesigned the icons to better understand their meaning, and ussed a color scheme to rapidly understand the properties of the plants, as they often use it in the fields. A webdesign was also created to transfer all those informations on the net, allowing farmers and other people to have better understanding of the eco-system they live in.
