Les ateliers Esus

Client : les ateliers Esus, Mr. Bajard
Année : 2016
Type de projet : Branding, Web design, Front-end development

Team project during University where a client asked us to concept his branding and website for a future FabLab & co-working place under the name "Les Ateliers d'Esus". We were 3 graphic designers and 2 developpers, and i acted as a Front-end developper in charge of the integration of the design produced by the other members of the team. The back-office was designed under Symfony2, while i used Bootstrap3, a tool i had never used at that time. The website allows people to read articles, learn about the different possibilities of this fablab and buy some time of access to the tools, but also classes where you learn to make an item for A to Z in one go. They can for example be purchased as a gift. The team designed the visual identity and all related items, such as the online communication on social media, youtube channels... but also designed some accessories and offline communication to advertise for the place. The entire project took place in under four weeks, from nothing but a vague idea of concept to the complete 360° package.
